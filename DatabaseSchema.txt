KumariCinemas database schema (final)

Tables (12)

CUSTOMER
- CUSTOMER_ID (PK)
- CUSTOMER_NAME
- CUSTOMER_CONTACT
- USERNAME
- ADDRESS

THEATRE
- THEATRE_ID (PK)
- THEATRE_NAME

THEATRE_CITY_HALL
- CITYHALL_ID (PK)
- THEATRE_ID (FK -> THEATRE.THEATRE_ID)
- CITYHALL_NAME
- CITYHALL_LOCATION

MOVIE
- MOVIE_ID (PK)
- MOVIE_TITLE
- DURATION
- MOVIE_LANGUAGE
- MOVIE_GENRE
- RELEASE_DATE

CITYHALL_MOVIE
- CITYHALL_ID (FK -> THEATRE_CITY_HALL.CITYHALL_ID)
- MOVIE_ID (FK -> MOVIE.MOVIE_ID)

SHOW
- SHOW_ID (PK)
- CITYHALL_ID (FK -> THEATRE_CITY_HALL.CITYHALL_ID)
- MOVIE_ID (FK -> MOVIE.MOVIE_ID)
- SHOW_DATE
- SHOW_TIME

SHOW_PRICE
- PRICING_ID (PK)
- SHOW_ID (FK -> SHOW.SHOW_ID)
- SHOW_PRICE
- PRICING_REASON

SEAT
- SEAT_ID (PK)
- CITYHALL_ID (FK -> THEATRE_CITY_HALL.CITYHALL_ID)
- SEAT_NO
- SEAT_STATUS

SHOW_SEAT
- SHOW_ID (FK -> SHOW.SHOW_ID)
- SEAT_ID (FK -> SEAT.SEAT_ID)

BOOKING
- BOOKING_ID (PK)
- CUSTOMER_ID (FK -> CUSTOMER.CUSTOMER_ID)
- SHOW_ID (FK -> SHOW.SHOW_ID)
- BOOKING_STATUS

PAYMENT
- PAYMENT_ID (PK)
- BOOKING_ID (FK -> BOOKING.BOOKING_ID)
- PAYMENT_STATUS
- PAYMENT_AMOUNT

TICKET
- TICKET_ID (PK)
- BOOKING_ID (FK -> BOOKING.BOOKING_ID)
- SEAT_ID (FK -> SEAT.SEAT_ID)
- TICKET_PRICE

Relationship rules

- One THEATRE has many THEATRE_CITY_HALL.
- One THEATRE_CITY_HALL has many SHOW.
- One THEATRE_CITY_HALL has many SEAT.
- One THEATRE_CITY_HALL can show many MOVIE rows through CITYHALL_MOVIE.
- One MOVIE has many SHOW.
- One SHOW has many SEAT through SHOW_SEAT.
- One CUSTOMER has many BOOKING.
- One BOOKING belongs to one CUSTOMER.
- One BOOKING belongs to one SHOW.
- One PAYMENT belongs to one BOOKING.
- One TICKET belongs to one BOOKING and one SEAT.

Critical direct foreign keys

- BOOKING.CUSTOMER_ID
- BOOKING.SHOW_ID
- PAYMENT.BOOKING_ID
- TICKET.BOOKING_ID
- SHOW.MOVIE_ID
- SEAT.CITYHALL_ID
